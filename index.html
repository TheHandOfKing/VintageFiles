NEED FOR SPEED MOST WANTED (2005)

From the creators of the Need for Speed Underground series, Need for Speed Most Wanted combines the thrill of street racing and tuner customization with the intensity of police pursuits.

Game Features :

The Blacklist

Go up against the most elite street racers of Rockport city. The Blacklist consists of 15 unique racers that will challenge your spot and skill, be ready to face them. Gain bounty, and finish milestones to earn their respect.

Updated Racing System

We went through our old games and the best out of each one, our new, and improved game engine and game physics simulate the real thrill of driving. Now supports handheld and a steering wheel.

New, Open World to Interact With

Our previous instalments had little to no open world contact, most of them just linear racing, but now prepeare to enjoy Rockport and the surroundings in their full glory. Appart from Rockport, we also made Camden Beach and Rosewood.

Smart And Improved AI

The AI is now programmed to do anything to win, from using shortcuts, calculating the best route or if you are being chased, place smart roadblocks and spikestrips along the road. The way to #1 will be extremley hard.

Full Range of Customization

The vast range of upgrades will make you immerse into the world of NFS. We provide upgrades for the whole car body, engine, nitrous, turbo and many other specifications.

Improved Game Physics

We took the time to test out any imperfections in our system, and made the best possible version of it, our testers, programmers and game designers spent extra time on their own behalf just to make this part of the game as realistic as possible.
