The Blacklist

Our newset addition, The Blacklist, is a newly profound feature, never before seen in our games. Challenge 15 elite members in many unique racing events. Here, on this page we will introduce you to some of the most memorable racers from this list.

The Blacklist is a list of racers in Rockport that are of high priority for capture by the Rockport Police Department and is organised by the amount of bounty placed upon them. The player​​ has to defeat each driver on the list to become the Most Wanted. There is a total of 15 racers with the main antagonist - Razor at the top of it.

Razor rocketed to the top of the blacklist using your ride. Now he's holding it down and rolling the streets like he owns them. He'll do anything he can to give himself an edge. That includes messin' with your head. Remember, you can't trust anything he says.
